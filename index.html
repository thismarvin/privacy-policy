Privacy Policy

Last updated on October 10th 2019

This privacy policy applies to all mobile games and applications developed by Team AM Squared.

Our applications are available for free on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We monetize our products through the use of advertisements and In-App Purchases that remove said advertisements. We strive to implement advertisements in a rewarding and non-obstructive matter.

Your privacy is very important to us; outlined below is an explanation of how our products handle your privacy.

Use and Collection of Personal Data

“Personal Data” is defined as any information that can be used to personally identify you. Our applications do not collect or store any personal data from any of our users.

  • Play Store In-App Purchases
